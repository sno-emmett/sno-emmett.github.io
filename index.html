Intro

ANF is the name of our new cloud server. To connect to ANF while outside of the office we will use a VPN application called Azure VPN. Azure VPN will also give you access to all of our current network drives. Once you have Azure VPN installed you will no longer use NetExtender.

Accept Invitation

You should receive an invite from Microsoft to connect to the cloud

Install Azure VPN

  1. Open the Microsoft Store app
  1. Search for Azure VPN client
  1. Click the Azure VPN Client card (pictured)
  1. On the next screen, install the app by clicking GET
  1. Once the app has been installed, launch the app by clicking OPEN

Connect to ANF

  1. When the Azure VPN Client has opened press ctrl + 'i'
  1. When the finder window appears, open azurevpnconfig.xml from C:\AzVPN üî∏
  1. A bunch of information will appear; click SAVE
  1. In ‚ÄúVPN connections‚Äù SnohettaANF should now appear (pictured)
  1. Click the CONNECT button located to the right of SnohettaANF
  1. You will be prompted to sign in with your Office365 account üü¢üî∫
  1. SnohettaANF should now say ‚ÄúConnected‚Äù and have a green dot. You are now connected to ANF! üü™
