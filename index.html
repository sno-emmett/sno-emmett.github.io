  1. Press Window + 'r' or search for Run in the Start Menu to launch the Run App (pictured left)

3. Install Azure VPN

  1. Open the Microsoft Store app
  1. Search for Azure VPN client
  1. Click the Azure VPN Client card (pictured)
  1. On the next screen, install the app by clicking GET
  1. Once the app has been installed, launch the app by clicking OPEN

4. Connect to ANF

  1. When the Azure VPN Client has opened press ctrl + 'i'
  1. When the finder window appears, open azurevpnconfig.xml from C:\AzVPN 🔸
  1. A bunch of information will appear; click SAVE
  1. In “VPN connections” SnohettaANF should now appear (pictured)
  1. Click the CONNECT button located to the right of SnohettaANF
  1. You will be prompted to sign in with your Office365 account 🟢🔺
  1. SnohettaANF should now say “Connected” and have a green dot. You are now connected to ANF! 🟪

Additional Info / Comments

🔬

ANF stands for “Azure Netapp Files”!

🛑

Azure VPN is a complete replacement for NetExtender. You will no longer be using NetExtender.

🏢

When working from the office you will be connected to ANF automatically without using Azure VPN. You are able to use Azure VPN while in the office, but it will slow your connection down. 🔷

Unlike NetExtender, If you close Azure VPN’s window it will disconnect you from ANF. Azure VPN will stay minimized on your taskbar while in use.

🏝

If you are working remotely for a stretch of days you may want to connect to Azure VPN automatically. To connect to Azure VPN automatically:

  1. Make sure your VPN is disconnected.
  1. Open your Internet Connections from the Taskbar
  1. At the top of your Internet Connections click SnohettaANF
  1. A window will open, click SnohettaANF
  1. Click the checkbox labeled “Connect Automatically”

Q. When I click CONNECT and attempt to sign in, the sign-in window disappears. In the “Status Logs” box it says, “…The operation was canceled by the user."

A. Complete the following:

  1. Open Windows Device Manager
  1. Navigate to Network adapters
  1. Uninstall all of the “WAN Miniport (XXXX)” devices
  1. Right-click on any item and choose to Scan for hardware changes
  1. Confirm the WAN Miniport devices have reappeared
  1. Return to step 6 of Setup The ANF Profile!

🟪

Q. I received an error popup when attempting to connect to SnohettaANF.

A. Complete the following:

  1. Close the popup and attempt to connect again. Sometimes the connection fails on the first try.
  1. If the above fails, visit IT or take a screenshot of the error and email snus-it@snohetta.com

Q. I’m connected to the office internet but my [letter] drive won’t load.

A. Try the following:

  1. Check that you are connected to the internet via WIFI (SNUS!) or Ethernet, but not both. Your WIFI should be disabled when connected to Ethernet.
  1. Shut your computer down fully, then reboot. This is not the same as restarting your computer.
  1. Ask your neighbor if they are having the same problem.
  1. If steps 1-2 don’t fix the problem, visit IT or email snus-it@snohetta.com and include the result of step 3
